About Us

History

Our company name is "Watch it Outside Events". We plan film festivals in many parts of the country all year long. Now we have thought of holding Boston Film Festival, "Watch it Outside Boston Edition" - as we like to call it. We expect to show films from 6th to 8th August in Boston Public Garden.

Our Founder

Clara Dunn is the Founder and Director of "Watch it Outside Events Co." The company has held many successful events under her directorship. She is a hard-working individual and wants to spread happiness all around the world through organizing film festivals.

Our Mission

Our mission is plain and simple and that is to "Bring the people together".

About the Festival

The Boston Film Festival endeavors to present a program of films that entertain, enlighten and educate the audience. We are dedicated to supporting evolving filmmakers by providing a forum to showcase their artistic vision. A variety of awards are presented annually, i.e. Best Film, Performance, Documentary, and Lifetime Achievement. New categories have been added to laud productions that influence green industry living in addition to the unique "Mass Impact Award" given to a filmmaker whose movie illuminates a social issue that positively affects humanity. The festival offers audiences first-hand access to the filmmakers and filmmaking process during the Q&A sessions with the Director and talent following every screening. The BFF continues its commitment to being a valued member of the city's cultural community contributing to the desirability of residing and working in the Bay State. Further, a rich history of programming illustrious films and featuring top actors elevates the international visibility of the region via publicity.
